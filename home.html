Understanding Non-Transmissible Diseases and Obesity

This book offers an in-depth exploration of non-transmissible diseases (NTDs) and their intricate connection to obesity. It covers the causes, prevention strategies, and management techniques for conditions such as cardiovascular disease, diabetes, and certain cancers, which are often influenced by lifestyle choices like poor diet and lack of exercise. Additionally, it addresses the growing obesity epidemic, providing valuable insights into its public health implications and practical solutions for individuals and communities alike.
